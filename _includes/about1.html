Finishing

Hot-Dip Galvanising

This method of galvanising is the most cost-efficient for ensuring the protection against rusting of the metal structure. The strong, silvery galvanisation will coat throughout the structure, leaving no area exposed to natural element.

Epdxy Paint Spray

Selecting the right type of paint is crucial in ensuring the lasting working quality of our products. Our air-spray epoxy painting not only protects the metal, it also gives a colour option for aesthetic purposes.

Raw Finish

This is also a special option for perserving the raw look of our metal products for both aesthetic consideration and experimental approach. Consult us for your finishing needs.
